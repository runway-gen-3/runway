Cookie Policy for Runway Digital Art Platform

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Runway (runway.alpha-gen3.com) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website about digital art, neural networks, and AI design.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Enhance user experience
  • Collect anonymous usage data
  • Personalize content related to digital art and AI technologies

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: Enable core website functionality
  • Examples:
    • Session authentication
    • Security protection
    • Form submission management

3.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Purpose: Understand user interaction with digital art content
  • Tools Used:
    • Google Analytics
    • Matomo Analytics
  • Data Collected:
    • Page views
    • User engagement
    • Traffic sources

3.3 Marketing Cookies

  • Purpose: Personalize advertising for digital art and AI design content
  • Tools:
    • Google Ads
    • Facebook Pixel
  • Data Used:
    • User preferences
    • Content interaction history

3.4 Preference Cookies

  • Purpose: Remember user settings and preferences
  • Examples:
    • Language selection
    • Theme preferences
    • Accessibility settings

4. Cookie Management

4.1 Consent Mechanism

  • Explicit consent required before non-essential cookies are activated
  • One-click cookie preference management
  • Granular control over cookie categories

4.2 How to Manage Cookies

Browser Settings

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies

Third-Party Tools

  • Cookie consent platforms
  • Browser extensions for cookie management

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may place cookies, including:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms

6. Cookie Retention Periods

  • Essential Cookies: Session duration
  • Analytics Cookies: 12 months
  • Marketing Cookies: 13 months
  • Preference Cookies: 6 months

7. User Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent
  • Request cookie data deletion
  • Object to data processing
  • Request cookie usage limitations
